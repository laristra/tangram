Material polygons in cell on either side of material polygon face.

Parameters
matface_idLocal ID of material polygon face in the cell
matpoly_id0Local ID of material polygon behind the face i.e. away from the normal (can be -1)
matpoly_id1Local ID of material polygon in front of the face i.e. in the direction of the normal (can be -1)

In 1D/2D, matpoly_id0 is to the left of the matface and matpoly_id1 is to the right

CAVEAT: We cannot retrieve matpolys across cell boundaries
